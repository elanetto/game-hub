About

Read about us - who work in your favourite gameshop!

About us
Game Hub is a webshop where you can buy the latest releases of games and also sell your old pre-loved games.
Whether you're looking for the hottest new releases or want to declutter your collection, Game Hub has you covered.
Our team
Our team consists of young, affectionate gamers who are passionate about spreading the love of gaming with the world.
We work tirelessly to keep you up to date on what's hot in the gaming world and recommend the best games for you to play.
Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, our team is here to ensure you have the ultimate gaming experience.
Our main base
Our main base is in an office.
We know, that means that we do not have an actual store for you to browse in.
However, this does mean that we have an excellent webshop for you - open 24/7!
